About Us

Welcome to the beginning of a community-driven journey to revitalize Crumpsall Community Hall.


As we stand at the threshold of this exciting venture, we're exploring the potential of establishing a co-operative model dedicated to the repair and sustainable operation of this cherished community asset.

Crumpsall Community Hall has been a cornerstone of our neighborhood, hosting countless events and memories. However, to continue its legacy, it needs our collective effort and care. The idea of a co-operative presents a unique opportunity for community ownership and involvement, ensuring the hall serves and represents the interests of Crumpsall's residents.

This initiative is still in its infancy, and we believe that the foundation of its success lies in the diverse contributions of our community members. Whether it's offering time, skills, resources, or moral support, every bit of assistance is invaluable.

We're eager to hear from anyone interested in joining this exploratory phase. Your ideas, suggestions, and involvement could help shape the future of the Crumpsall Community Hall and, by extension, our community at large.

Get Involved

Crumpsall Community Co-operative is more than a project; it's a movement towards creating a sustainable future for the Crumpsall Community Hall, powered by the strength and spirit of our local community.

Your involvement can make a significant difference.

We are calling on all community members who share our vision for a revitalized, vibrant Crumpsall Community Hall to come forward. Whether you can offer time, skills, resources, or simply your voice in support, there's a place for you in this endeavor.

How You Can Help

  • Volunteer: Lend your hands and hearts in various capacities, from repair work to event organization.
  • Donate: Financial contributions help fund renovations, programs, and maintenance of the hall.
  • Spread the Word: Amplify our cause by sharing it within your network and on social media.
  • Participate: Attend events, meetings, and workshops to stay informed and involved.

Every action, no matter the size, propels us closer to our goal. Together, we can transform Crumpsall Community Hall into a thriving hub of activity, culture, and connection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Crumpsall Community Co-operative is an initiative aimed at fostering community engagement and managing the Crumpsall Community Hall.

Crumpsall Community Hall is located on Cleveland Road, Crumpsall, M8 4GS, Manchester.

There are several ways to get involved with the Crumpsall Community Co-operative, including volunteering for repair and renovation projects, participating in organizational meetings, contributing ideas for community programs, and making financial donations to support our efforts. Everyone's contribution is valuable, and we welcome diverse skills and perspectives.
